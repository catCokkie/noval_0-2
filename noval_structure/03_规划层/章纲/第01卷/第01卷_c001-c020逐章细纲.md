## c001-c003

### c001
- **Purpose**: establish work-state, social position, and first anomaly
- **Key Beats**:
  - maintenance-shift routine in the mining belt
  - dorm fatigue, ration logic, tool use, work hierarchy
  - minor raw-value anomaly during routine inspection
  - reflected/body mismatch subtly amplifies unease
  - pressure-well emergency escalates fast
  - protagonist trusts physical trace over expected workflow
  - outcome record becomes irreconcilable with what he actually did
- **Pressure Increase**: routine labor becomes life-risk emergency
- **Evidence Movement**: raw values, action, and system record split three ways
- **Reveal Ceiling**: body truth hidden; only technical and bodily irregularity

### c002
- **Purpose**: turn accident into containment pressure
- **Key Beats**:
  - injury, worker fear, rumor, and silence spread through the site
  - reporting and verification procedure begins
  - official explanation starts narrowing the event
  - protagonist notices mismatch between procedure and reality
  - he makes his first private verification move instead of open protest
- **Pressure Increase**: accident becomes institutional pressure chamber
- **Evidence Movement**: he preserves the possibility that the official account is false
- **Reveal Ceiling**: anomaly treated as technical irregularity, not possession

### c003
- **Purpose**: prove the incident is not isolated
- **Key Beats**:
  - a witness/worker tied to the accident disappears
  - search moves through dorms, ration lines, rumor channels, and maintenance residue
  - signs appear that the person is being erased from the practical record chain
  - local fear becomes targeted fear rather than general panic
  - protagonist concludes this is a repeatable structure, not one bad shift
- **Pressure Increase**: truth-preservers can vanish
- **Evidence Movement**: event upgrades from accident to pattern
- **Reveal Ceiling**: no explicit Black Falcon co-speaking
