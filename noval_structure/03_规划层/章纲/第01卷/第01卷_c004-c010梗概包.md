# Volume 1 c004-c010 Synopsis Package

## Purpose
- consolidate the post-golden-three early arc into one usable package
- bridge the opening hook into the first sustained phase of pressure, pattern recognition, and deliberate probing

## Strategic Function
- these chapters must transform the protagonist from a person who has seen contradiction into a person who can no longer treat contradiction as local or accidental
- the arc should do five things:
  1. make curiosity materially costly
  2. make contradiction objective across sites and records
  3. make workplace pressure vertical rather than only social
  4. make bodily anomaly tactically useful and therefore frightening
  5. end with the protagonist's first deliberate probe

## Arc Summary

### c004-c005 - Curiosity Becomes Expensive
- the missing-witness line continues, but now under daily labor pressure
- workers begin protecting themselves by distancing, warning, or resenting the protagonist
- food, shifts, trust, and ordinary survival become entangled with silence
- the system produces a response/reward that feels too precise or too large for a low-tier worker's visible behavior
- the protagonist begins to suspect that the system is tracking something deeper than labor output

### c006-c007 - Contradiction Leaves the Original Site
- reassignment or secondary duty carries the protagonist to another site
- there he encounters the same kind of falseness in altered form: residue, delay, receipt mismatch, cleanup rhythm, or wording pattern
- this is where contradiction stops being personal intuition and becomes objective process-form
- by the end of `c007`, reality feels administratively bendable across locations

### c008-c009 - The Workplace Turns Hostile
- pressure from above becomes visible through warnings, reassignment, scrutiny, or surveillance
- attached labor cases show how ordinary people are disciplined into compliance through material vulnerability
- at the same time, bodily anomaly becomes more useful in practical survival
- that usefulness creates a second fear: what keeps the protagonist alive may also be what marks him out

### c010 - First Deliberate Probe
- the protagonist faces a threshold situation where passive observation is no longer enough
- he performs a surface-compliant action while preserving a second truth-line underneath it
- the move proves that the system/order can be probed, but the cost lands immediately
- he exits the chapter not as a passive witness, but as someone who has chosen dangerous knowledge-work

## Pressure Curve
- `c004`: curiosity threatens routine stability
- `c005`: curiosity threatens belonging
- `c006`: contradiction expands geographically
- `c007`: contradiction becomes objective
- `c008`: authority pressure becomes explicit
- `c009`: self becomes part of the contradiction
- `c010`: the protagonist acts deliberately against managed truth

## Reveal Discipline
- still no explicit Black Falcon naming or full co-speaking
- body truth remains hidden
- system hostility remains inferential rather than openly declared
- allowed escalation:
  - excessive system precision
  - pattern recurrence across sites
  - worksite targeting pressure
  - tactical bodily usefulness
  - intentional preservation of alternate truth

## Drafting Priorities

### What Must Be Felt
- social cost of asking wrong questions
- widening scale of contradiction
- institutional presence pressing downward
- growing unease that the protagonist himself is part of the pattern
- the moral shift from noticing to choosing

### What Must Not Happen Too Early
- full dual-soul explanation
- open anti-system manifesto
- large external-faction takeover of the plot
- detached side quests that stop feeding the mine/pressure-well spine
