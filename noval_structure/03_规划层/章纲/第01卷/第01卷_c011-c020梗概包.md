# Volume 1 c011-c020 Synopsis Package

## Purpose
- consolidate the closing movement of the opening twenty chapters into one usable package
- carry the early arc from suspicion and probing into consequential anomaly, structured deception, and first durable doubt

## Strategic Function
- these chapters must convert unstable suspicion into committed pursuit
- the arc should do five things:
  1. make continued verification increasingly costly
  2. turn bodily anomaly from eerie detail into consequential event-factor
  3. expose official deception as structured management
  4. stage the near-loss of a true chain
  5. end with a first local truth preserved against flattening

## Arc Summary

### c011-c013 - Suspicion Becomes Chosen Risk
- fallout from the first deliberate probe changes the protagonist's practical reality
- hidden verification grows harder to sustain under scrutiny
- material penalties and worker-side consequences show that doubt costs food, trust, rest, and survival margin
- by `c013`, the protagonist is no longer drifting into truth; he is knowingly staying on the line

### c014-c016 - Bodily Anomaly Becomes Consequential
- a threshold crisis forces the first truly consequential internal override
- the anomaly proves tactically useful but lands immediate physical, social, or evidential cost
- aftermath chapters bind proof to self-doubt: the protagonist gains stronger reason to distrust the system, while also trusting himself less
- by `c016`, the contradiction no longer lives only in the workplace; it lives inside him

### c017-c018 - Deception Gains Architecture
- a key official or institutional voice reveals inconsistency across context, audience, or record layer
- the protagonist realizes the lie is not sloppy but policy-shaped
- attached social material shows that falsehood stabilizes through both administration and ordinary life
- the event is not merely hidden; it is versioned, layered, and managed

### c019-c020 - First Small Victory / First Durable Doubt
- one true chain comes under direct threat of erasure
- the protagonist takes a meaningful risk to preserve enough of it
- he does not expose the whole system, but he prevents one local truth from being completely flattened
- by `c020`, the system can no longer honestly be treated as neutral

## Pressure Curve
- `c011`: safety margin shrinks
- `c012`: doubt costs material stability
- `c013`: risk becomes chosen
- `c014`: anomaly acts
- `c015`: anomaly costs
- `c016`: self becomes evidence
- `c017`: official truth cracks
- `c018`: truth is shown to be layered and managed
- `c019`: true chain nearly dies
- `c020`: one true chain survives

## Reveal Discipline
- Black Falcon remains a hostile/internal claim, not a fully available partner
- no full body-truth reveal
- no full disaster-system explanation
- allowed escalation:
  - tactical bodily override
  - altered pain threshold / split-action effects
  - record-layer inconsistency
  - partial proof that deception is structured
  - local preservation of truth

## Drafting Priorities

### What Must Be Felt
- fatigue of carrying hidden verification
- fear that survival and selfhood are both under pressure
- increasing visibility to authority
- the ugliness of managed truth
- the relief and terror of preserving one real thing

### What Must Not Happen Too Early
- clean internal alliance with Black Falcon
- public proof that changes the whole site/system immediately
- full institutional confession
- collapse of all ambiguity around the protagonist's body and origin
