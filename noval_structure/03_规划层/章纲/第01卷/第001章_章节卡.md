# Volume 1 c001 Chapter Card

## Chapter Role
- opening chapter of Volume 1
- establishes the labor-world lens, the protagonist's niche competence, first technical anomaly, first bodily mismatch, and first hard contradiction
- must function as both world-entry and hook chapter

## Chapter Goal
- make the reader feel that this industrial world is real, lived-in, and oppressive
- make the protagonist feel observant, capable, and slightly wrong in a way he cannot explain
- end on a contradiction strong enough that the story cannot collapse back into ordinary accident logic

## Golden-Chapter Requirements
- strong opening grounded in work, not exposition
- visible world texture: labor, food/ration logic, gear, hierarchy, fatigue, environment
- protagonist makes one distinctly "his" judgment
- chapter ends with a hard contradiction: action vs raw values vs system record

## Reveal Ceiling
- no explicit body-truth reveal
- no named Black Falcon explanation
- no full system-conspiracy exposition
- allowed:
  - bodily mismatch
  - mirrored/reflected irregularity
  - unexplained precision or unease
  - system record contradiction

## Dominant Contradiction in This Chapter
- **physical trace / raw reality** vs **workflow / expected interpretation**

## Chapter Outcome
- protagonist survives or helps stabilize the first pressure-well emergency by trusting trace over expected process
- the final record does not match what actually happened
- reader exits knowing this is not merely a dangerous workplace, but a world where reality and logged truth already diverge

## Scene Card Sequence

### Scene 1 - Shift Entry
- **Purpose**: open with immediate labor reality
- **What to Show**:
  - shift change mood
  - fatigue and bodily wear
  - ration leftovers / meal memory / hunger logic
  - gear, grime, temperature, metallic smell, ghost-steam atmosphere
  - workplace hierarchy and the protagonist's low-tier position
- **Character Function**:
  - protagonist is competent in a narrow, practical way
  - he is not socially central
- **Pressure Level**: low
- **Must Avoid**: lore dumping about factions or system history

### Scene 2 - Routine Inspection
- **Purpose**: establish the protagonist as a technical observer
- **What to Show**:
  - normal maintenance check procedure
  - how he reads raw equipment state rather than just trusting expected outputs
  - a minor raw-value anomaly that should be explainable
- **Character Function**:
  - he notices the kind of thing others wave off
- **Pressure Level**: low -> mild unease
- **Evidence Seed**:
  - first raw-value inconsistency

### Scene 3 - Reflected Mismatch
- **Purpose**: weave bodily wrongness into technical wrongness
- **What to Show**:
  - reflection in metal, water, gauge glass, or polished surface
  - subtle timing mismatch in hand, gaze, or body position
  - protagonist suppresses the moment instead of narratively explaining it
- **Character Function**:
  - he already knows he is "not normal" in some vague way
  - he has learned not to overreact publicly
- **Pressure Level**: mild internal alarm
- **Must Avoid**: explicit internal dialogue with Black Falcon

### Scene 4 - Escalation Trigger
- **Purpose**: turn routine into emergent danger
- **What to Show**:
  - pressure-well instability or adjacent mechanical escalation
  - sound, heat, vibration, team reaction, procedural commands
  - the chain of command moving faster than full understanding
- **Character Function**:
  - protagonist must choose what to trust under time pressure
- **Pressure Level**: emergency
- **Evidence Seed**:
  - raw values diverge sharply from expected handling logic

### Scene 5 - Distinctive Decision
- **Purpose**: make the protagonist act in a uniquely “his” way
- **What to Show**:
  - he trusts physical trace / raw state over the smoother official or expected path
  - the choice is risky and explainable only after the fact
  - bodily irregularity and tactical competence overlap without clarification
- **Character Function**:
  - establishes his core mode: not heroic bravado, but stubborn trace-trust
- **Pressure Level**: peak
- **Must Avoid**: making him superhuman or already fully system-savvy

### Scene 6 - Hard Contradiction Ending
- **Purpose**: deliver the chapter hook and launch the book
- **What to Show**:
  - after the event, compare:
    - what he did
    - what the raw values indicated
    - what the system record or result says happened
  - the three do not align
- **Character Function**:
  - he cannot honestly smooth the event into ordinary procedure anymore
- **Pressure Level**: conceptual rupture
- **End Requirement**:
  - chapter must end on a concrete contradiction, not mood alone

## Evidence Checklist

- [ ] bodily mismatch echoes technical mismatch
- [ ] main incident creates a sharper contradiction
- [ ] end-of-chapter contradiction is concrete and memorable

## Expansion Notes

- if expanded into prose next, strengthen sensory labor detail before increasing metaphysical unease
- if chapter becomes too dense, cut background explanation before cutting contradiction logic
