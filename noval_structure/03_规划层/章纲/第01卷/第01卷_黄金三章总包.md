# Volume 1 Golden Three Package

## Purpose
- consolidate `c001-c003` into a single handoff-ready opening package
- provide a compact reference for drafting, review, and continuity checks

## Strategic Function
- the first three chapters must do five things together:
  1. establish the labor-world as lived and concrete
  2. establish the protagonist as observant, capable, and slightly wrong
  3. make the first technical/bodily anomaly memorable
  4. convert accident into managed truth
  5. upgrade local danger into systemic danger

## Opening Arc Summary

### c001 - Work, Wrongness, and First Contradiction
- opens inside ordinary maintenance labor rather than exposition
- introduces fatigue, ration logic, gear, hierarchy, industrial atmosphere, and the protagonist's narrow technical competence
- embeds a small raw-value anomaly inside routine work
- lightly echoes that anomaly through bodily/reflected mismatch
- escalates into the first pressure-well emergency
- ends on a hard contradiction between action, raw values, and system record

### c002 - Procedure, Narrowing, and Private Verification
- converts the accident into paperwork, rumor, institutional language, and containment pressure
- shows how workers metabolize danger by adapting to sanctioned narrative
- makes procedure itself a dramatic mechanism
- gives the protagonist his first private verification act
- ends on the realization that the event is being actively narrowed or rewritten

### c003 - Disappearance, Erasure, and Structural Recognition
- begins with a missing witness/worker as practical absence
- uses worker spaces to widen the world without losing the main line
- reveals that disappearance can happen administratively as well as physically
- ties the attached case back to the original accident through echoing detail
- ends by upgrading one event into a repeatable structure of managed truth

## Escalation Logic
- `c001`: danger is physical + epistemic
- `c002`: danger becomes institutional
- `c003`: danger becomes systemic

## Reveal Discipline
- no explicit Black Falcon naming or co-speaking
- no body-truth reveal
- no full system-conspiracy explanation
- allowed opening-layer signals:
  - bodily mismatch
  - mirrored irregularity
  - excessive system precision
  - record contradiction
  - practical erasure of people and traces

## Drafting Priorities

### What Must Be Felt
- labor fatigue
- low-tier precarity
- procedural pressure
- protagonist's trace-trust mindset
- growing instability between lived truth and managed truth

### What Must Not Happen Too Early
- explicit metaphysical explanation
- broad faction infodump
- investigator/higher-order plot dominance
- full dual-soul framing
