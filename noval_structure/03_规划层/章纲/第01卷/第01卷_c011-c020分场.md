# Volume 1 c011-c020 Scene Breakdown

## Scope
- scene-level planning for chapters `c011-c020`
- completes the first twenty-chapter opening arc at the same planning granularity as `c001-c010`
- preserve current rules:
  - dominant contradiction = `real record vs official explanation`
  - Black Falcon remains a hostile/internal claim, not a fully explained partner
  - `c020` must end with durable doubt, not full revelation

## Global Guardrails
- these chapters must convert suspicion into commitment
- internal anomaly must become consequential, but not fully interpretable
- institutional deception must read as structured management, not random sloppiness
- first small victory must preserve one true chain, not solve the whole system

---

## c011 Scene Sequence

### Scene 1 - Immediate Probe Fallout
- **Function**: show that c010 changed the protagonist's practical reality
- **Content**:
  - aftermath of the first deliberate probe
  - work routines feel less safe and less neutral than before
  - small but clear signs that he is now under greater notice
- **Pressure**: his room for error contracts

### Scene 2 - Hidden Verification Under Strain
- **Function**: keep the evidence line alive while making it costly
- **Content**:
  - protagonist tries to maintain private checking habits under worse conditions
  - he is forced to choose what to verify and what to let go
- **Evidence Move**: hidden verification remains possible but unstable

### Scene 3 - End on Reduced Safety Margin
- **Function**: close the chapter with the feeling that ordinary life is no longer a refuge
- **End Requirement**: the reader should feel that the protagonist has entered a narrower corridor of survival

## c012 Scene Sequence

### Scene 1 - Material Penalty Lands
- **Function**: make suspicion expensive in concrete terms
- **Content**:
  - ration cut, shift downgrade, tool restriction, bed reassignment, or work allocation pressure
  - the penalty should be believable inside labor-world procedure
- **Pressure**: curiosity now costs food, rest, trust, or bodily safety

### Scene 2 - Worker Consequence Attached Case
- **Function**: show the system punishes the vulnerable indirectly
- **Content**:
  - another worker pays for a small deviation or becomes collateral in the managed narrative
  - protagonist sees how ordinary people are disciplined into silence
- **Pressure**: resistance looks socially contagious and therefore dangerous

### Scene 3 - End on Practical Loss
- **Function**: convert abstract unease into a lived setback
- **Evidence Move**: official records become less usable while survival costs rise

## c013 Scene Sequence

### Scene 1 - Safety vs Evidence Decision
- **Function**: force the protagonist to choose whether to continue
- **Content**:
  - he can retreat into safer compliance or keep the line alive
  - no option is clean
- **Pressure**: inquiry becomes a deliberate gamble, not accidental drift

### Scene 2 - Controlled Continuation
- **Function**: show a more disciplined, conscious version of his curiosity
- **Content**:
  - he chooses one narrow thread to keep instead of trying to hold everything
  - this makes him feel more strategic and more alone
- **Evidence Move**: survival and evidence become competing budgets

### Scene 3 - End on Chosen Risk
- **Function**: mark the moral turn
- **End Requirement**: the chapter ends with the sense that he knowingly stays on the line

## c014 Scene Sequence

### Scene 1 - Threshold Crisis
- **Function**: create the moment where internal anomaly becomes operationally visible
- **Content**:
  - sudden danger or tactical compression forces a response faster than ordinary conscious control can provide
- **Pressure**: protagonist cannot solve this with planning alone

### Scene 2 - Consequential Internal Override
- **Function**: make bodily/internal irregularity decisive
- **Content**:
  - a split-action, altered pain threshold, or hostile-but-effective override saves the situation or changes the outcome
  - the act should feel useful and violating at once
- **Evidence Move**: body anomaly becomes linked to observable event consequence
- **Reveal Limit**: no fully framed second-person conversation or identity reveal

### Scene 3 - Immediate Cost
- **Function**: deny the anomaly any clean heroic framing
- **Content**:
  - physical, social, or evidential cost lands fast
  - someone notices something, or something gets harder to explain
- **Pressure**: what saves him also endangers him

- **Evidence Move**: proof increases but so does uncertainty about the self

## c019 Scene Sequence

### Scene 1 - One True Chain Under Threat
- **Function**: stage the near-loss of something real
- **Content**:
  - a sample, witness fragment, receipt mismatch, tool trace, or hidden note is about to be taken, sealed, erased, or destroyed
- **Pressure**: the truth line becomes materially fragile

### Scene 2 - Risk to Preserve
- **Function**: force a meaningful protective act
- **Content**:
  - protagonist chooses to risk position, safety, or explainability to keep the chain alive
- **Evidence Move**: near-loss turns into conditional preservation

### Scene 3 - End on Partial Salvage
- **Function**: close with strain rather than triumph
- **End Requirement**: he saves enough, but not cleanly

## c020 Scene Sequence

### Scene 1 - Reconstruct the True Chain
- **Function**: convert fragments into first durable doubt
- **Content**:
  - protagonist connects enough surviving pieces to justify continued risk
  - this should not feel omniscient; it is a narrow but real reconstruction
- **Pressure**: commitment replaces drifting suspicion

### Scene 2 - Small Victory, Not Resolution
- **Function**: give the opening arc its payoff without collapsing the larger mystery
- **Content**:
  - the system/order fails to flatten one chain completely
  - protagonist wins a local truth, not a public truth
- **Evidence Move**: one durable true chain survives the first arc

### Scene 3 - End on Chosen Continuation
- **Function**: close the first twenty-chapter movement
- **Content**:
  - protagonist can no longer honestly treat the system as neutral
  - he now knows enough to keep going, and enough to fear continuing
- **Reveal Ceiling**: system not neutral; deeper body/system truth deferred
