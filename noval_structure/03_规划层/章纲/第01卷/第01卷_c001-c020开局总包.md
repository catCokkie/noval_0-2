# Volume 1 c001-c020 Opening Arc Package

## Purpose
- provide a single entrypoint for the full opening twenty-chapter arc
- unify the first twenty chapters into one operational package for drafting, review, and continuity control

## Strategic Function
- this opening arc must move the story through three linked states:
  1. **anomaly appears**
  2. **contradiction spreads and hardens**
  3. **doubt becomes chosen commitment**

- by `c020`, the protagonist must no longer be able to honestly treat the system as a neutral industrial aid

## Arc Division

### Segment I - Golden Three (`c001-c003`)
- function: hook the book and establish the world from below
- key achievements:
  - labor-world feels concrete and oppressive
  - protagonist is competent, guarded, and slightly wrong
  - first technical anomaly and bodily mismatch arrive together
  - accident becomes managed truth
  - one local event upgrades into systemic danger
- package file: `\.sisyphus/plans/volume-1-golden-three-package.md`

### Segment II - Escalation and Probe (`c004-c010`)
- function: turn contradiction from private suspicion into objective pattern
- key achievements:
  - curiosity becomes materially and socially expensive
  - contradiction appears across sites and records
  - workplace pressure becomes explicit from above
  - bodily anomaly becomes tactically useful and frightening
  - protagonist performs the first deliberate probe
- package file: `\.sisyphus/plans/volume-1-c004-c010-synopsis-package.md`

### Segment III - Cost, Structure, and First Durable Doubt (`c011-c020`)
- function: turn unstable suspicion into committed pursuit
- key achievements:
  - verification becomes costly
  - bodily anomaly becomes consequential
  - official deception becomes visibly structured
  - one true chain nearly dies but survives locally
  - first small victory produces durable doubt
- package file: `\.sisyphus/plans/volume-1-c011-c020-synopsis-package.md`

## Opening-Arc End State
- protagonist has moved from uneasy technical observer to active, risk-accepting truth-preserver
- the system is no longer merely suspicious; it is now a contested and non-neutral force
- Black Falcon remains implicit, hostile, and consequential, but still not fully explained
- the industrial order has been shown to manage truth through both procedure and social survival pressure

## Master Pressure Curve
- `c001-c003`: physical + epistemic danger
- `c004-c007`: contradiction becomes larger than one site
- `c008-c010`: power notices and pressure descends
- `c011-c016`: survival, selfhood, and anomaly start colliding
- `c017-c020`: deception gains architecture and one true chain survives

## Reveal Discipline
- do not reveal body truth in this arc
- do not convert Black Falcon into a clean ally in this arc
- do not let external factions dominate before the western industrial order is fully established as the social window
- do not let local victory become public victory

## Core Feelings To Preserve
- labor fatigue
- low-tier precarity
- procedural suffocation
- suspicion turning into deliberate knowledge-work
- bodily usefulness that feels violating rather than empowering
- relief mixed with fear when truth survives at all
