# Volume 1 c004-c010 Scene Breakdown

## Scope
- scene-level planning for chapters `c004-c010`
- this section deepens the opening arc after the golden three chapters
- preserve current rules:
  - dominant contradiction = `real record vs official explanation`
  - attached cases must feed the main mine/pressure-well spine
  - Black Falcon remains implicit and tactically consequential, not fully explained

## Global Guardrails
- these chapters must deepen danger, not simply repeat suspicion
- every attached case must return something to the main incident spine:
  - residue
  - witness pattern
  - altered record practice
  - institutional language
  - system-response anomaly
- by `c010`, protagonist must cross from witness to experimenter

## c004 Scene Sequence

### Scene 1 - Continuing Search Under Daily Pressure
- **Function**: show that the missing-witness thread persists inside ordinary work constraints
- **Content**:
  - protagonist tries to ask questions while still performing shift duties
  - work supervisors or peers signal that continued curiosity is costly
- **Pressure**: normal labor and private investigation now compete directly

### Scene 2 - Social Friction in the Worker Layer
- **Function**: show worker fear and self-protective silence
- **Content**:
  - some workers avoid him, some warn him, some subtly resent him for stirring danger
  - practical stakes like shifts, food, work trust, and future assignments appear
- **Pressure**: social ground starts thinning beneath him

### Scene 3 - Excessive System Response
- **Function**: introduce the first reward/response that feels disproportionate
- **Content**:
  - a system reaction grants too much precision, reward, or implied attention for a low-tier labor outcome
  - protagonist registers that the system may be tracking hidden variables
- **Pressure**: success itself becomes suspicious
- **Evidence Move**: system values more than official work logic admits

### Scene 4 - End on Unearned Precision
- **Function**: leave chapter with the sense that the system is reading deeper than it should
- **End Requirement**: chapter should close on unease about why the system noticed him so specifically

## c010 Scene Sequence

### Scene 1 - Threshold Situation
- **Function**: create the scenario for first deliberate probe
- **Content**:
  - a task, site, or verification point appears where he can either remain passive or test the system/order boundary
- **Pressure**: choice is now conscious

### Scene 2 - Surface-Compliant Action
- **Function**: establish cortex-level compliance behavior
- **Content**:
  - he performs the expected, explainable action at the visible level
- **Pressure**: if this fails, he is exposed immediately

### Scene 3 - Hidden Second Truth-Line
- **Function**: preserve or generate alternative evidence underneath compliance
- **Content**:
  - he secretly keeps, redirects, marks, or verifies something that the official path would flatten
- **Evidence Move**: two truth-lines coexist by intent, not accident

### Scene 4 - Cost and Confirmation
- **Function**: complete the threshold
- **Content**:
  - the move works enough to prove the system can be probed
  - but the price lands immediately in social, institutional, or bodily form
- **End Requirement**: protagonist exits c010 no longer as a passive witness, but as someone who has chosen dangerous knowledge-work
