- help choose between drafting, further outlining, and canon reconciliation without losing momentum

## Best Immediate Writing Starts

- **Primary files**:
  - `\.sisyphus/plans/volume-1-c001-chapter-card.md`
  - `\.sisyphus/plans/volume-1-c001-synopsis.md`
- **Why this is the best first prose test**:

  - labor-world opening is stable

- **Use when**:

### 2. Golden-Three Consecutive Drafting Start
- **Primary files**:
  - `\.sisyphus/plans/volume-1-golden-three-package.md`
  - `\.sisyphus/plans/volume-1-c001-synopsis.md`
  - `\.sisyphus/plans/volume-1-c002-synopsis.md`
  - `\.sisyphus/plans/volume-1-c003-synopsis.md`
- **Why this is the best structural drafting start**:
  - the first three chapters already form a complete opening promise
  - escalation is clear: anomaly -> narrowing -> systemic danger
  - continuity across the opening will be easier to manage than writing isolated sample chapters
- **Use when**:
  - you want to validate the novel's opening engine rather than a single chapter's style only

### 3. Opening-Arc Continuity Start
- **Primary files**:
  - `\.sisyphus/plans/volume-1-c001-c020-opening-arc-package.md`
  - `\.sisyphus/plans/volume-1-c001-c020-outline.md`
- **Why this is the best planning-before-drafting start**:
  - ensures the opening twenty chapters hold one coherent pressure curve
  - best for continuity review or multi-role collaboration before prose expansion
- **Use when**:
  - you want consistency first and prose second

- **If the goal is team coordination / canon stability**: use the `c001-c020` opening-arc package before drafting further
