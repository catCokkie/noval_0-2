- Existing canon already supports western industrial host-faction, container language, low-rank sequence exposure, and trust-to-doubt arc.
- Existing files still contain older assumptions that can conflict with the new brief.
- Complexity risk is real; Volume 1 must subordinate world density to one social window and one dominant contradiction.

- Added locked/provisional/deferred decision ledger.
